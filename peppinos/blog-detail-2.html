Fermented Foods for Gut Health

Our dosas and idlis are made from fermented batter, which creates beneficial probiotics that support digestive health. The fermentation process also increases the bioavailability of nutrients, making them easier for your body to absorb.

Chef Laddi explains: "The traditional fermentation process not only gives our dishes their unique tangy flavor but also transforms simple ingredients into nutritional powerhouses."

Plant-Based Protein Power

Our separate vegetarian kitchen specializes in dishes rich in plant-based proteins from lentils, legumes, and grains. These combinations provide complete amino acid profiles while being easier to digest than many animal proteins.
