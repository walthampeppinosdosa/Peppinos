Traditional Spice Blends: The Heart of Indian Cuisine

At Peppino's Dosa, our secret lies in the carefully crafted spice blends that have been passed down through generations. Chef Peppino and Chef Laddi share their knowledge about the traditional masalas that make our dishes extraordinary.

The Art of Spice Blending

Each spice blend in our kitchen serves a specific purpose. Our garam masala combines warming spices like cardamom, cinnamon, and cloves, while our sambar powder brings together the earthy flavors of coriander, cumin, and fenugreek.

"The key is in the roasting," explains Chef Peppino. "Each spice must be roasted to its perfect point to release its essential oils and create the complex flavors our customers love."

Fresh Ground Daily

We grind our spices fresh daily in small batches to ensure maximum flavor and potency. This commitment to freshness is what sets our dishes apart and keeps our customers coming back for more.
