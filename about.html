Our Story

Authentic Indian Dining Experience

Peppino's Dosa casual eatery in Waltham, where Chef Peppino along with Chef Laddi bring you authentic North and South Indian cooking paired with excellent service and a wonderful ambiance to create a very unique Indian dining experience. One place, two tasty worlds. Craving plant-based? Prefer something meaty? We've got you covered. Our unique setup features separate kitchens-one for fresh vegan and vegetarian dishes, the other for flavorful meat options.
